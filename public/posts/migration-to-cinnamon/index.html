Migration to Cinnamon

May 4, 2024

A couple of months ago, I switched to Cinnamon on my gaming desktop. As I wrote in other articles, I am using a secondary hard drive on my desktop PC to acomplish my Linux tasks as I have Windows 11 on my primary drive. I do not have an intention of removing Windows 11 on the primary drive as I also have some programs that may not run on Linux.

I am using Arch Linux on the secondary drive. KDE Plasma 6 completely shattered my luck by borking my sci-fi KDE desktop setup. So, I tried various other desktop environments and

Qtile

I have been using Qtile for a while until I stopped using it since tiling window managers are not for me. I riced my Qtile setup with a sci-fi wallpaper, rofl with a theme to make it look nice, and polybar to make Qtile at home. It was easier said than done.

Cinnamon

I am now using Cinnamon as a desktop environment.

The disadvantage is the way Qt applications play with my desktop environment. I have not found a Qt theme that would integrate with my Cinnamon desktop setup.
